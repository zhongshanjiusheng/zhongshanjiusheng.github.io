About Us

Zhongshan Jiusheng Photoelectric Technology, started in 2010, was established in Zhongshan City, Guangdong Province, which is the China Lighting Center. For more than 10 years, we focus on the R&D, production and sales of smart LED lamps.

After years of research and development, we have multiple product lines such as:

  • Aquarium lights
  • Hydroponic lights
  • Smart lamps
  • Indoor ceiling lights
  • Wall lights
  • Engineering spotlights
  • Outdoor solar lights

We have our brand WOINO, and have obtained 10 international patents and Chinese patents, with more than 5 copyrights. Our company is firmly among the best in the industry.

Over the past 10 years, based on the market, customer-oriented, we continuously innovate, committed to improving the function and power consumption of LED lamps. We continuously improve the convenience and reliability of installation and use, allowing smart LED lamps to gradually replace traditional lamps and opening a new era of smart lighting.
